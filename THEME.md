# Luxury Minimal Theme

(Based on `theme-factory` skill format)

A sophisticated, high-end aesthetic that combines warm neutrals with stark typography and subtle organic textures. Designed to evoke premium SaaS quality without generic tech tropes.

## Color Palette

- **Luxury White**: `#ffffff` - Primary surface
- **Sand**: `#f5f5f5` - Secondary background / warmth
- **Charcoal**: `#1a1a1a` - Primary text and strong accents
- **Deep Navy**: `#0a192f` - Very deep accent (rare use)
- **Accent Gold**: `#d4af37` - Highlights and focus states

## Typography

- **Headers**: Syne (Display) - Bold, wide, characterful.
- **Body Text**: Manrope (Sans) - Clean, legible, modern geometric.

## Texture

- **Noise**: Subtle SVG noise overlay (4% opacity) to add tactility.
- **Glass**: Frosted glass effect for navigation and floating elements.

## Best Used For

High-end SaaS landing pages, boutique agencies, luxury product showcases, and portfolios requiring a clean, confident statement.
